The IRS sets the allowable limit for deductible mileage, if used for business or commuting purposes, and that amount is currently $0.56 per mile (currently IRS has no separate cost factor for electric vehicles). XYZ ABC mileage rates are fixed for all riders and drivers, and tiers down from $0.20 as a method to equitably share the cost of a journey, assuming 2-3 persons per car.
